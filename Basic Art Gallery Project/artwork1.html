The Glow of Hope

S.L Haldankar

Portrait Artist

$12,800

Artwork Details

Medium: Acrylic on Canvas

Year: 1945-1946

Subject: Gita Haldankar (now Smt. Gita Krishnakant Uplekar)e

Framed: Yes, Floating Frame

About the Artwork

Glow of Hope, alternately titled "Woman With the Lamp".

The painting shows an Indian woman holding a lighted lamp in one hand, the other hand shielding the light of the lamp from the front.The woman is in a simple and traditional Indian saree. The effect of the painting is heightened by the shadow of the woman in the back.

About the Artist

Sawlaram Haldankar, born in a princely state now part of Maharashtra, was a talented artist who studied at Sir J.J. School of Art and later founded his own institute and the Art Society of India , gaining international recognition as a watercolorist and portrait painter. His famous painting "Glow of Hope" features his daughter Gita, who lived to be 102.

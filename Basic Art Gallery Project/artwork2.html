Artwork Details

Subject: Shakuntala

Framed: Yes, Ornate Frame

About the Artwork

Shakuntala, one of Raja Ravi Verma's most celebrated masterpieces, depicts the moment when Shakuntala, the heroine from the Mahabharata, is lost in thoughts of her beloved King Dushyanta. The painting captures Shakuntala’s beauty and emotional turmoil as she gazes at the royal ring, unaware that the king has forgotten her due to a curse.

The detailed portrayal of her emotions, the lush greenery surrounding her, and the intricate folds of her traditional attire are hallmark features of Verma’s style.

About the Artist

Raja Ravi Verma, an iconic figure in Indian art, is known for bringing Indian mythology to life through his paintings. His works, blending traditional Indian themes with European techniques, have left an indelible mark on Indian art. Verma’s *Shakuntala* exemplifies his mastery of portraying human emotion and storytelling through art.
